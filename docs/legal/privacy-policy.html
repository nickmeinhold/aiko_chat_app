Aiko Chat — Privacy Policy

Effective: 30 June 2026. Last updated: 30 June 2026.

Aiko Chat is a messaging app. This policy explains, in plain language, exactly what data the app handles and what it does not. The short version: we collect only your account identity and the messages you send, we use them only to run the chat service, and we do not track you, run ads, sell your data, or include any third-party analytics of any kind.

1. Who we are

Aiko Chat ("the app", "we") is operated by Imagineering. The app connects to our own server ("the gateway") to deliver messages. We are the data controller for the information described below. Contact details are in §10.

2. What we collect

We collect only what the app needs to function. There is no advertising identifier, no location data, no contacts access, no browsing history, and no third-party analytics or tracking SDK in the app.

We do not collect: precise or coarse location, contacts, photos/media beyond what you choose to send, health or financial data, advertising identifiers, or any cross-app tracking signal.

3. How we use it

  • To provide the core service: authenticate you, deliver your messages, and sync your history across reconnections.
  • To keep the service safe: operate the in-app blocking and reporting features, and act on reports of abuse in line with our terms.
  • To maintain security and reliability of the service.

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling for ads, or sale to third parties, and we do not build cross-app tracking profiles.

4. Who we share it with

We do not sell your personal data and we do not share it for advertising. Limited, purpose-bound sharing happens only with:

RecipientWhat & why
Sign-in providers (Apple, Google)When you choose them to sign in, they handle authentication and return a verified identifier (and, with your permission, email/name). Their handling is governed by Apple's and Google's own privacy policies.
Other usersMessages you send are delivered to the people and channels you send them to. Your display name is visible to people you chat with.
Legal & safetyWe may disclose information if required by law, or where necessary to investigate abuse or protect the rights and safety of users.

5. Storage, security & retention

  • In transit: all communication between the app and our server uses encrypted connections (HTTPS / secure WebSocket, TLS).
  • On your device: authentication tokens are held in the platform secure store (iOS Keychain / Android Keystore); your message cache is stored in the app's private storage.
  • On our server: your account record and your messages are stored to provide chat history and delivery.
  • Retention: we keep your account data and messages for as long as your account exists. When you delete your account (see §6), your account and associated message data are deleted.

6. Your choices & rights

  • Account & data deletion: you can delete your account from within the app at any time, which removes your account and associated data from our service.
  • Blocking & reporting: you can block other users and report content from within the app.
  • Access & correction: depending on your region (e.g. GDPR/UK GDPR, CCPA), you may have rights to access, correct, or delete your personal data, and to object to certain processing. Contact us (§10) to exercise these.

7. Children

Aiko Chat is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from them. If you believe a child has provided us personal data, contact us and we will delete it.

8. International users

Our servers may process and store data in locations that differ from your own. By using the app you understand that your information may be transferred to and processed in those locations under appropriate safeguards.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above and, where appropriate, an in-app notice. Continued use after an update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

10. Contact

Questions or privacy requests: privacy@imagineering.cc.
