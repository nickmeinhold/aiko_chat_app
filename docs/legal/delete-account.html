Delete your Aiko Chat account

How to permanently delete your Aiko Chat account and the data associated with it.

You can delete your account at any time. Deletion removes your account and its associated data — your account identity (the email/name from your sign-in method) and your messages — from our service.

Option 1 — delete from within the app (fastest)

Open Aiko Chat → Settings → Delete Account, then confirm. Your account and associated data are deleted from our service.

Option 2 — request deletion by email

If you can no longer access the app, email us from the address associated with your account:

To: privacy@imagineering.cc
Subject: Delete my Aiko Chat account

We will verify the request comes from the account owner and process the deletion.

What is deleted, and when

  • Deleted: your account record (account identity, email/display name) and your messages.
  • Timeframe: deletion is processed promptly; email requests are actioned within 30 days of verification.
  • Retention exceptions: we may retain limited records where required by law; these are not used for any other purpose and are deleted when no longer legally required.

See also our Privacy Policy. Questions: privacy@imagineering.cc.
