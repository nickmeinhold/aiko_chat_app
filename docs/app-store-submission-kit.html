Notes for Review (the important one) 
This app uses social sign-in only (Sign in with Apple and Google) — there is
no username/password, so no demo credentials are provided. To sign in, tap
"Sign in with Apple" on the login screen and authenticate with any Apple ID;
a Aiko account is created automatically.

UGC compliance: a EULA must be accepted on first launch (zero tolerance for
objectionable content). Users can block other users and report messages from
a long-press menu. Account deletion is available in Settings → Delete Account.
