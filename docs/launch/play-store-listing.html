Full description (max 4000 chars)

Aiko Chat is a clean, private messaging app built on one idea: your conversations are yours. No ads. No third-party trackers. No analytics SDKs quietly watching how you use the app. Aiko Chat collects only what it needs to work — your account and your messages — and nothing more. WHAT YOU GET • Fast, simple messaging organised into channels • Modern sign-in with Apple or Google — nothing new to remember • Block and report tools, so you stay in control of who you talk to • Delete your account and your data at any time, right from the app PRIVATE BY DEFAULT We don't show ads, we don't sell your data, and we don't embed third-party tracking or analytics. Your messages are encrypted in transit between the app and our servers. You can read exactly what we collect — and what we don't — in our privacy policy: https://imagineering.cc/aiko/privacy/ Want your data gone? Delete your account in-app, or request deletion any time: https://imagineering.cc/aiko/delete-account/ Aiko Chat is in active development and improving quickly. We'd love your feedback.
