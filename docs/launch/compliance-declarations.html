3. Google — Data Safety form

Play Console → your app → Policy → App content → Data safety.

  • Does your app collect or share any of the required user data types? → Yes (collect).
  • Is all collected data encrypted in transit? → Yes.
  • Do you provide a way for users to request that their data be deleted? → Yes (in-app account deletion). grounded: task #8 shipped.

"Shared" in Play's sense = transferred to a third party. The gateway is your own first-party server, and the sign-in providers act on the user's instruction to authenticate — so No sharing is the correct answer. Declare no App activity / App info-and-performance types (no analytics/crash SDK).

5. #1447 — Play App Signing SHA-1 (unblocks Google sign-in on the Play build)

Google Sign-In on the Play-delivered build fails with DEVELOPER_ERROR (code 10) because the OAuth Android client only knows the debug SHA-1. The installed build is re-signed by Play App Signing, so two fingerprints must be registered on the Android OAuth client.

Console: Google Cloud → APIs & Services → Credentials (project 588100172166) → open the Android OAuth client for package cc.imagineering.aiko_chat_app → add SHA-1 fingerprints.
